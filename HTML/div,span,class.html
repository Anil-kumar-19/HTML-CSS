About

new terms and concepts are required. The most important being the concept of chirality. A chiral object is one that cannot be superposed on its mirror image. The term originates from the greek term for “hand” As it is with human hands, the left hand cannot be superimposed on the right hand. It is the same with chiral molecules. They are non superimposable
